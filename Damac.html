OUR EXPLORE PORJECT

 
DAMAC Casa

DAMAC Casa is an ethereal escape that transcends the boundaries of urban living, offering a serene island high above Dubai's sprawling metropolis. Rising majestically, DAMAC Casa boasts a fluid avant-garde design that seamlessly integrates into Dubai's extensive cityscape. Nestled in Al Sufouh, the tower presents breathtaking views of the iconic Palm Jumeirah and mesmerising sea views. The tower features apartments and townhouses, with one- and two-bedroom townhouses situated on the property's first and second levels. On higher levels, you can savour the vibrancy of city life while enjoying the tranquillity of elevated living in 1- and 2-bedroom luxury apartments. For those seeking sky-high island luxury, explore the 3-, 4-, and 5-bedroom super luxury apartments. The podium rooftop features an artificial beach and a multitude of cabanas, and hydrotherapy amenities, including an oasis lap pool. Immerse yourself in an island-styled pool, ascend to new heights of relaxation at the chillout lounge and on sky floating beds, and discover unique aqua art installations that create a sensory experience like no other.

GOLF GATE 2

Nestled amidst the breath-taking landscape of DAMAC Hills in Dubai, Golf Gate 2 offers a truly exceptional and luxurious living experience that combines modern comfort with the serenity of nature. The stunning tower offers panoramic views of manicured fairways of a world-class golf course providing residents a sense of tranquillity that is unrivalled in the bustling city of Dubai. The luxury apartments here are a testament to impeccable design and boast of thoughtfully curated details.

Golf Gate 2 residents will enjoy the convenience and benefits of being part of DAMAC Hills, one of Dubai's most sought-after communities. Here, you'll have access to a host of world-class amenities and facilities, including schools, supermarkets, pools, and recreational spaces, offering families the opportunity to lead a life of comfort, leisure, and well-being.

THE FARMHOUSE

At The Farmhouses, experience the joy of country living in a thriving community that invites you to surround yourself in verdant beauty and tranquility.

Rediscover nature's symphony and fragrant delights as you escape the city's hustle, and indulge in lush green landscapes that replace cityscapes. Embrace eco-living with ample space to grow your own fruits and vegetables, and ride into adventure on horseback. Immerse yourself in the charm of European style country homes reminiscent of the peaceful meadows of the Netherlands and Belgium or indulge in the grandeur of massive North American-style country homes, the epitome of frontier living.

The beauty of the farmhouse is that it can be anything you want it to be.

 
 
MOROCCO LAGOONS)

Discover the final chapter of Morocco at DAMAC Lagoons, where timeless heritage and luxury intertwine in a mosaic of wonders.

Embrace the magic of Moroccan living in luxury villas and townhouses that harmoniously blend traditional elements with modern design. From intricate Zellij tilework to Moorish arches, every detail has been carefully crafted to capture Morocco’s rich tapestry of art and architecture.

An oasis of tranquility, Morocco at DAMAC Lagoons offers a peaceful retreat enveloped with an abundance of wellness activities. Indulge in the age-old richness of argan oil treatments amidst verdant hills or find calm in the outdoor yoga area. Relax at the Serenity Garden adorned with traditional rugs and explore the cactus garden inspired by Marrakech's Jardin Majorelle. And when you want to savour the tastes of Morocco, unwind at the floating atay stations and quench up at the orange clementine café.

Journey through this timeless yet modern haven, which beats to the sounds, colours and culture of Morocco.
